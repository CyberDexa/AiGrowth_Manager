Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

1. Introduction

AI Growth Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our social media management platform.

2. Information We Collect

When you use AI Growth Manager, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information from your social media accounts (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook/Instagram)
  • Social Media Data: Posts, analytics, follower counts, and engagement metrics from connected accounts
  • Content: Content you create, schedule, or publish through our platform
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform and features you use

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our social media management services
  • Authenticate you via OAuth (LinkedIn, Twitter, Meta)
  • Post content on your behalf to your social media accounts
  • Display analytics and insights about your social media performance
  • Generate AI-powered content suggestions and strategies
  • Communicate with you about our services

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your social media credentials with anyone
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes outside our platform

We may share information with:

  • Social Media Platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter, Meta (as authorized by you via OAuth)
  • Service Providers: Clerk (authentication), Render.com (hosting), OpenRouter (AI services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Render.com cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. Social media access tokens are encrypted before storage. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Clerk: User authentication and session management
  • LinkedIn API: For LinkedIn OAuth and posting
  • Twitter API: For Twitter OAuth and posting
  • Meta API: For Facebook/Instagram OAuth and posting
  • OpenRouter: For AI-powered content generation
  • Render.com: Cloud hosting and database

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Revoke social media account connections at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out of communications from us
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you disconnect a social media account or delete your account, associated data is removed within 30 days unless legally required to retain it.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using our service, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
